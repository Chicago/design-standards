Welcome to the Chicago Design System

The Chicago Design System is the public identity of the City of Chicago.

It is an inclusive, equitable, cost-effective system for City communications and expression of the public's love for Chicago.

Our new public identity's strength is its ability to reimagine Chicago's history and heraldry for the 21st century.
