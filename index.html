Welcome to the Chicago Design System

The Chicago Design System is the public identity of the City of Chicago.

Its purpose is for all peoples to see themselves in the symbols of our City and have pride of place in being a Chicagoan. It is an inclusive, cost-effective update of our public identity for the 21st century, built on the history of Chicago and our rich civic heraldry.
